About Us

Stevenson's Academy is a learning institution that dose'nt only educate her people academically, we equally instill in them the necessary knowledge required to live a life driven by purpose.

We believe that every child born to this world has a purpose tied to their existence here on earth; We have made it our mission, and goal to help our students discover the reason with which they came to this world, which can only be done with the help of the one who made them.
